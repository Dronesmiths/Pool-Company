Why Spas Need Different Treatment

Spas aren't just small pools ‚Äî they operate at higher temperatures, have different water flow dynamics, and require more frequent chemical adjustments. Our technicians understand these differences and treat each feature appropriately.

Different Chemistry Balance

Hot water requires different pH, alkalinity, and sanitizer levels than pools. We manage each feature's chemistry separately for optimal results.

Pool + Spa Service FAQs

Is the spa cleaned every week?

Yes. Both pool and spa receive full service during the same weekly visit. Your technician handles both features to maintain optimal water quality.

Do you drain and refill spas?

Spa drain-and-refill service is available as an add-on every 3-4 months as needed. We'll coordinate scheduling when it's time for fresh water.

What if I only use one feature?

Both features still need weekly maintenance even if not actively used. Stagnant water develops problems quickly in our desert climate.

Can I add spa service later?

Absolutely. If you currently have pool-only service, we can upgrade to combo service anytime. Just give us a call.
