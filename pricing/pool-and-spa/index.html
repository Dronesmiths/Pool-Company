Pool + Spa Service FAQs

Is the spa cleaned every week?

Yes. Both pool and spa are serviced during the same weekly visit. Your technician handles both features to keep water balanced and clean.

Are spa chemicals different?

Yes. Spas run at higher temperatures and require different chemical balancing than pools. We adjust each feature separately for optimal results.

What if only one needs service?

We still service both features weekly. Regular maintenance prevents issues, even if you're not actively using one of them.

Can I add spa service later?

Absolutely. If you start with pool-only service, we can easily upgrade to combo service anytime. Just let us know.

Do you drain and refill spas?

Spa draining and refills are an additional service performed every 3-4 months as needed. We'll coordinate scheduling when it's time.
