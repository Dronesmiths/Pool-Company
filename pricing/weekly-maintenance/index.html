What Affects Your Monthly Price?

While most pools fall within our standard pricing tiers, a few factors may adjust your final monthly rate:

  • Pool Size: Larger pools require more chemicals and cleaning time.
  • Pool Shape: Complex shapes or multiple levels take longer to service.
  • Debris Load: Properties with heavy tree coverage need extra attention.
  • Equipment Type: Salt systems, DE filters, or heated pools may require specialized care.
  • Condition at Start: Neglected pools may need extra visits to restore balance.

Weekly Maintenance FAQs

Are chemicals included?

Yes. All standard pool chemicals (chlorine, pH adjusters, alkalinity balancers) are included in your monthly service price.

What day will you come?

We schedule your pool on a specific day each week and stick to it. Consistency helps maintain water quality and makes service predictable.

Do I need to be home?

No. As long as we can access your pool, you don't need to be home. We'll leave a service tag confirming the work completed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. We don't lock you into long-term contracts. Service continues as long as you're satisfied with our work.

What if my pool turns green?

With consistent weekly service, green pools are extremely rare. If a problem does occur, we handle it quickly at no extra charge for our regular customers.
