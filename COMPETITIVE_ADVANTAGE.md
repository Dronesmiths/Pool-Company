To dominate the market, the site must explicitly communicate why AV Pool Bros is the superior choice over local competitors. Use this roadmap to define and deploy these 5 "Unfair Advantages."

## 1. The "Big 5" Differentiators
Define 5 specific reasons why customers choose this brand.
- **Advantage 1: Weekly Reliability** - "We show up every week, same time, every time. No missed visits."
- **Advantage 2: Transparent Chemical Pricing** - "No hidden 'shock' fees. All core chemicals included in your monthly rate."
- **Advantage 3: Expert Equipment Repair** - "We don't just clean; we fix pumps, filters, and salt cells on-site."

## 2. Competitive Positioning (The "Anti-Commodity" Play)
- **Identify the "Generic" Competitor**: What do others do poorly? (e.g., "They skip weeks", "Water stays cloudy").
- **The Contrast Copy**: For every "They do X", we write "We do Y".

## 3. Deployment Locations

- **Homepage "Why Choose Us"**: The primary grid of 3-5 cards with high-fidelity icons.
- **Service Pages**: Custom "Pro Tip" or "The AV Pool Bros Difference" sidebars.
- **About Page**: Deep-dive into the standards and vetting process.
- **Blog Pillars**: Articles like "5 Things Your Pool Company Won't Tell You."
